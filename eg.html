Our Vision

لطف ني وات ؛ كه مرثية انسس قصيدة نسس نظم كظظوائي ؛، نظم يعني سوطط؟ كه جوڑائيلو، تو مرثية انسس قصيدة ما ايك بند بيسرا بند سي جوڑائيلو ؛، انسس اهنا ضمن ما گهني چيزو جوڑائيلي ؛، يه سگلي چيزو جمع تهائي ؛ تو يه نظم كظظوائي ؛

Insha- un nazm is the vision to bring more and more mumineen living across the world nearer to Aqa Maula TUS. Just the way one misra is in someway joint with every band and every band further explains the tasawwar behind the misra, we wish and hope to join and unite mumineen by bringing them closer to Aqa Maula TUS.

We urge to motivate and meaningfuly help them to pen down their tasawwar in the purest way known-Zikr and Madeh. We know how hard it can be to figure out a lehen and to serach all around for possible qafiyahs- BUT NO MORE!.

Through our website each and every mumin will be now able to find a list of Qafiyahs for their desired lehen, and with that part done being all that left is madeh!.
